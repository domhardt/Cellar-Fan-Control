# Cellar-Fan-Control Mechanics

### BOM

- parts to obtain (for a setup with 1 fan and one filter)
    - fan
        - EBM-PAPST 4650N
        - 1 piece (buy more for a multifan setup)
    - WiFi-enabled microcontroller
        - WeMos D1 Mini
        - 1 piece
    - humidity and temperature sensors
        - BOSCH BME280
        - 2 pieces
    - TASMOTA-enabled WiFi socket
        - SONOFF S26 or SONOFF Basic
        - 1 piece (buy more for a multifan setup)
    - white UV-resistant filament for the Stevenson Screens
        - ASA or a UV-resistant PLA variant (ABS, PETG and most PLA filaments are not UV-resistant)
        - e.g. Extrudr PLA NX2 https://www.extrudr.com/en/products/catalogue/pla-nx2-wei_1964/
        - approx. 250g
    - fileament to print all the other mechanical parts
        - can be the same as the Stevenson Screens but regular PLA will do
        - approx. 250g (approx. 150g more for each additional fan mount, aprox. 85g more for each additional filter)
    
    - filter material
        - filter class G2 or better
        - 150 mm x 150 mm x 5 mm (filter can be as thick as 10 mm)
        - 1 piece (this filter should be replaced once each year or so)

- 3D printed parts
    - Fan Mount
        - own design using Fusion 360
        - printed in PLA
    - Filter Mount
        - own design using Fusion 360
        - printed in PLA
    - Stevenson Screen
        - This thing was created by Thingiverse user ORMER, and is licensed under Creative Commons - Attribution - Non-Commercial - Share Alike.
        - https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3222598
        - printed in UV-resistant white PLA
    - WeMos D1 Mini Case
        - This thing was created by Thingiverse user NoGue, and is licensed under cc-nc-nd.
        - https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:5229342
        - printed in PLA
